about

Hello there! I first want to thank you for visiting my website. My name is Azhar. I am currently a Cloud Engineering student, working towards my AWS Cloud Practitioner certificate. I am now looking for a Junior Cloud Engineering position.

I have always had an interest for problem solving and in these modern times technology is at the forefront of solving the problems we have in life no matter how big or small. With this I am also a creative person in that I love creating things and seeing the end result of my creation and watching others wnjoy it too. Similar to writing lines of code to create a programme making sure it is correct and links to the other lines of code. Then when you test it and it does exactly what you want it to, the feeling is incredible and more incredible when others test it and the same result happens.

In my journey in tech so far I have attended a 3 week course through CodeNation where I learnt how to construct a website and further enhanced my coding skills. I have completed the AWS re/start programme remotely where I learnt the fundamentals of IT and Cloud Computing amongst other skills. I will continue to practice what I have learnt and develop my skills further.
